Sampaguita Restaurant Menu

Sampaguita is a fictional Filipino fusion restaurant. It is a high end restaurant that evokes a sophisticated and aristocratic vibe that would remind you of old Manila.

The goal of the project was to design a menu with two versions. One is an online interactive menu that follows accessibility rules, and the other is a printed menu with a specialty finish.
