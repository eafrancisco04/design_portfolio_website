WWF Ad Campaign

The World Wildlife Foundation (WWF) is an international organization that aims to bring awareness and reduce human impact on the environment. They also do work for the protection of wild animals and indigenous groups. WWF operates in over 100 countries and is the leading international conservation organization in the world.

The goal of the project was to make 3 ad campaigns that are independent of each other but are also in the same theme.
