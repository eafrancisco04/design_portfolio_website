Time Magazine Editorial Design

For this project, the goal was to design an editorial article that will be printed on a magazine that we deem appropriate. The article I chose focuses on edible grubs and insects.

TIME is the magazine that I chose because it is an educational and informative magazine. The editorial is supposed to follow the magazine's guidelines and should have the same branding as it. In this case, I chose to publish it as a special edition, hence the lack of the red frame.
