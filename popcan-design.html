Pop Can Design

Splash! is a fictional soda company that makes carbonated fizzy drinks. Their branding is more on bright colors, fruity patterns, and a tropical feel.

The goal of this project was to create a pop can design and create your own branding for it. We also had to place it in a 3D model that we made in Illustrator. I created additional flavours to make it a set and reinforce the branding.
