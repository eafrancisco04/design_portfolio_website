Puerto Princesa Process Book

Puerto Princesa is a city in the province of Palawan, located in the Philippines. It is has beautiful natural forests and a diverse ecosystem. It is also the home of one of the Seven Wonders of Nature, which is the famous Palawan Underground River. The city's goal is to preserve its environment and to grow its economy through investments and tourism.

The goal of this project is to rebrand the city. To make it more modernized while also preserving its unique traits and values. this project contains various components and the process of its creation.
